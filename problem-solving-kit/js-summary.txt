1. Function: declaration, parameters, return, error handling, try-catch, throw, arguments validation, scope, promises, async-await, fetch. 

2. Variables: let, const, operations. 

3. Loops: for, while, forEach, nested loops, break, continue. 

4. Flow control: if, else if, else, nested if, operators, return, early return, operations and order of operations.  

5. Arrays: forEach, map; splice, modify one element, push-unshift-pop-shift; filter, find, includes; sort; spread operator; destructuring; assign a value to an element. 

6. Objects: dot notation, bracket notation; destructuring, spread, object.keys, object.values, object.entries combined with for...of. 

7. Strings: split, repeat, toLowerCase-toUpperCase, trim, template literals.
